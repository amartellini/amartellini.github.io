Emotional prosody in the human brain

Leading a study targeting emotional prosody in the brain; designing tasks, working with professional actors to create experimental stimuli, collecting behavioral data, running fMRI scans, and analyzing outcomes. (Fedorenko Lab, MIT.)

Embodied emotion & autonomic regulation

Designed experiments based on a literature review in the science of emotion and brain-body interaction, culminating in a successful $3M grant focused on embodied emotion research in primates. (Jazayeri Lab, MIT/HHMI.)

Bayesian modeling of risk behavior (BART)

Precision oncology decision support

Projects

Selected research & builds.

Emotional prosody in the human brain

fMRI + behavioral study mapping how prosodic cues convey affect; designing tasks, running scans, and analyzing outcomes. (Fedorenko Lab, MIT.)

Embodied emotion & autonomic regulation

Program-level work tying neural signals to physiological states; protocols, compliance, multi-team coordination. (Jazayeri Lab, MIT/HHMI.)

Bayesian modeling of risk behavior (BART)

Built hierarchical models, ran MCMC on multi-cohort data (n>300), and evaluated 30+ metrics; presented at Stanford’s Symbolic Systems Poster Fair.

Precision oncology decision support

Prototyped generative and reinforcement-learning approaches for patient-specific treatment recommendations; shared results with cross-functional teams.

Origins of orphan globular clusters.

Spectroscopic analysis and statistical classification; co-authored work presented at AAS 231.
